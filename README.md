# Tech Giants Thesis

## Research Objectives

- Compare financial performance of Apple Inc. (AAPL) and Microsoft Corporation (MSFT).
- Analyze stock trends, profitability, ownership structure, and analyst outlook.

## Methodology

1. Retrieve current stock information and market capitalization.
2. Collect daily historical price data (2023‑01‑01 to 2024‑12‑31) to compute period return and volatility.
3. Extract annual income statements for latest fiscal year to assess revenue and net income.
4. Obtain top institutional holders to understand ownership composition.
5. Compile analyst recommendations for Apple over the past 12 months.

All data and analysis are stored in this repository for review by the thesis committee.
